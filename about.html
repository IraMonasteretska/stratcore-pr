About us

Stratcore - a translation
company with a difference
Your one-stop supplier for language services

Stratcore provides translation services to companies within a broad spectrum of industries from the banking and finance, insurance, construction, telecommunications and IT sectors to the mechanical and automotive, pharmaceutical/biotechnological and the forestry industries to name a few. No job is too big – or too small for us. We maintain complete confidentiality in each and every contact with our customers, both internally and externally.

More than just a translation company

Alongside translations, we offer a full range of language services including website localisation, proofreading, interpretation, transcription, voiceovers, copywriting and intercultural advice.

Our vision

Stratcore’s goal: to establish a long-term and mutually rewarding relationship with you. Our personal service, professional staff and technical expertise ensure that you will always receive the highest quality translation. Stratcore—translating your world—a major presence on the Nordic market.

Our commitment

Stratcore is committed to communicating your message in a culturally correct, clear and concise way wherever you are and wherever you wish to do business. Our translation company is available 24/7 to serve your needs: send us your urgent translations – any time, any place. No matter how tight your schedule, our goal is always to deliver translations of the highest quality. We always go the extra mile for you.

Our policy

At Stratcore you always come first. We are flexible, friendly and ready to step in at short notice whenever you need us. Only when you are completely satisfied will we ever say mission accomplished.

Our translators

We don’t translate words; we translate culturally correct content in your chosen target language.  Stratcore works exclusively with in-country language professionals—skilled translators who live and work in the country where you want to target your message. Each translator focuses on a specific area of expertise, e.g. legal, finance, medical, technical, advertising, etc. This ensures high-quality translations regardless of the professional area. Whatever the language, we will source the right language professionals to communicate your message to your chosen market.

Our customers

In today’s economy, global businesses must choose a reliable, skilled, efficient and professional translation company to meet their ever-increasing global requirements. Companies choose Stratcore translation services because of our expertise, credibility, high-quality work, creativity – and our 24/7 support. Stratcore’s exacting quality assurance procedures – based on extensive experience and expertise – ensure that our best practice processes work, work well, and work in the best interests of our clients.

Our security policy and confidentiality

Stratcore strives to be a reliable and trustworthy translation service provider with the highest level of security meeting our customers’ justifiably stringent demands. At Stratcore, we all understand and accept the obligations implicit in our security policy; we have all signed a confidentiality agreement that expressly prohibits us from disclosing sensitive information we might have access to during the course of our work. We also employ special procedures for sensitive projects, including document storage and destruction. What’s more, our website is located on an external host server to ensure no visitors can access our office network. The fixed connection to our network used for email and the Internet is protected by a firewall certified by ICSA (International Computer Security Association).

Optimising strategic communication for our clients is core to our ethos Stratcore
